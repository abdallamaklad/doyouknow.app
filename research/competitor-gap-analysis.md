# doyouknow.app — Competitive Content Gap Analysis

**Research Date:** July 2026  
**Competitors Analyzed:** Time Out Dubai, Lovin Dubai, What's On Dubai, Visit Saudi, Arab News  
**Method:** 24+ targeted web searches (kimi_search_v2), directory analysis, content format extraction, social media strategy review  
**Output:** Content matrix, 50 ranked gaps, keyword suggestions, format recommendations, bilingual priorities, action plan

---

## 1. Executive Summary

doyouknow.app currently operates as a knowledge-explainer platform focused on **UAE/Saudi landmarks, government procedures, Vision 2030 mega-projects, and major sporting events** (World Cup 2026). Its content is predominantly **evergreen, educational, and SEO-optimized** with a bilingual EN/AR strategy. However, the site has **significant gaps in lifestyle, recurring events, dining/nightlife, weekly guides, and real-time community content** — areas where competitors dominate search traffic and social engagement. This analysis identifies **50 high-value content opportunities** ranked by search volume, competitive density, and strategic fit for doyouknow.app's "know more, live better" positioning.
