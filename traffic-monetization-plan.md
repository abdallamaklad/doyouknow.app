#### Stream 1: Display Ads (Month 4)

| Platform | Requirements | Expected RPM | Monthly Revenue @ 5K sessions |
|----------|-------------|-------------|------------------------------|
| **Google AdSense** | No minimum traffic | $3–$8 | $450–$1,200 |
| **Ezoic** | 10,000+ sessions/month | $8–$15 | $1,200–$2,250 |
| **Mediavine** | 50,000+ sessions/month | $15–$30 | — (future target) |

**Action**: Apply for Google AdSense at 3,000 sessions/month. Upgrade to Ezoic at 10,000. Target Mediavine at 50,000.

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|----------|
| **AI content detection** | Mandatory `humanizer` pass on all articles |
| **Google algorithm update** | Focus on E-E-A-T (experience, expertise, authority, trust) |
| **Traffic plateaus** | Diversify channels (social, email, paid, referral) |
| **Monetization too early** | Wait for 3,000+ sessions before ads; 5,000+ before affiliates |
| **Content quality drops** | Maintain 1,500+ word minimum, 5 FAQs, sources, internal links |
| **Arabic content underperforms** | A/B test Arabic vs English headlines; optimize for Arabic search behavior |
