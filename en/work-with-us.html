Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum sponsorship commitment?▼

Our Bronze package starts at $300 per sponsored article. There is no long-term commitment required for one-off sponsorships.

How long does it take to publish a sponsored article?▼

Typical turnaround is 7–10 business days from content approval. Rush delivery (3–5 days) is available for Gold and Platinum partners.

Can we provide our own content?▼

Yes. We accept client-provided drafts and will edit them for tone, fact-checking, and SEO optimization before publication.

Do you offer performance reports?▼

Yes. Silver, Gold, and Platinum packages include a performance summary with page views, engagement time, and social reach metrics.

Which payment methods do you accept?▼

We accept bank transfer, PayPal, and Wise. Invoices can be issued in USD, AED, or SAR.
