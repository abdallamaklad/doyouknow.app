Frequently Asked Questions

Do residents need a Dubai driving licence?

Many residents need a UAE/Dubai licence to drive legally, though exchange eligibility and requirements vary. Check RTA rules for your nationality and situation.

Is renting or buying better in Dubai?

Renting is easier for short stays and testing needs. Buying can make sense for longer residents with predictable commutes, parking, and maintenance budgets.

What is Salik in Dubai?

Salik is Dubai’s electronic toll system. Owned and rental cars may incur Salik charges when passing toll gates, billed according to the vehicle arrangement.

Is driving in Dubai difficult?

It is manageable for confident drivers, but highways are fast and lane changes can be intense. New drivers should start with familiar routes and avoid peak stress times.

Is this article legal advice?

No. It is a descriptive guide. Always confirm licence, insurance, registration, and traffic rules through official RTA, Dubai Police, or authorised service channels.
