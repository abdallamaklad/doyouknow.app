Frequently Asked Questions

When does the UAE school year 2026-27 start?

Many private schools start around August or September, but the exact date depends on emirate, regulator, curriculum, and individual school calendar. Check KHDA, ADEK, and the school directly.

Are UAE school fees regulated?

Private school fees are subject to regulator rules and approvals, but families should still budget for non-tuition costs such as transport, uniforms, books, exams, devices, and activities.

Do children need Emirates ID for school admission?

Schools commonly request Emirates ID, passport, residence visa, and health records, though new arrivals may submit some documents later depending on the school and regulator rules.

Which curriculum is best in the UAE?

There is no single best curriculum. Choose based on child needs, language, future university plans, transfer continuity, commute, and budget.

Is this education policy advice?

No. It is a planning guide. Confirm dates, fees, and admission rules with KHDA, ADEK, the Ministry of Education, and the school.
