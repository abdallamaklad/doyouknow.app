Conversion is not the same as starting from zero

Some residents can convert a valid foreign driving license to a UAE license without taking the full beginner training route. Others must open a driving file, take classes, and pass tests. The first question is whether your issuing country, nationality, residence emirate, license category, and document status qualify under the current authority rules.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can every foreign license be converted in the UAE?

No. Eligibility depends on current authority rules, issuing country, nationality, license category, and document validity.

Do I need an eye test?

Usually yes. Approved eye-test results are commonly part of the license issuance or conversion process.

Can I use an international driving permit as a UAE resident?

Do not assume so. Resident rules can differ from visitor rules. Confirm with the relevant transport authority.

What if my country is not eligible?

You may need to open a driving file, attend training, and pass theory and road tests.

Is this legal advice?

No. It is an editorial guide. Check RTA, UAE Government Portal, or the relevant emirate authority for current instructions.
